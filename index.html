About this event

SEADECK Saturdays

Set sail on Sydney Harbour for an unforgettable Saturday afternoon aboard SEADECK — where music, cocktails, and harbour views come together in perfect harmony.

From 12:30PM to 4:30PM, cruise through the heart of the city to the sounds of house, funk, and disco, brought to life by our resident DJ. Let the rhythm move you as you sip on signature cocktails and indulge in fresh, seasonal dishes from our à la carte menu — all while soaking in Sydney’s iconic skyline from the water.

Whether you're gathering the crew for a celebration or simply elevating your weekend plans, SEADECK Saturdays is the ultimate daytime escape.

Tickets are limited—secure yours now for a cruise to remember.

BOOKINGS ESSENTIALS

This is an 18+ event. All guests guest attending are required to show a valid form of ID upon arrival.

Please ensure you have valid photo ID to produce on boarding.

All tickets are non-refundable.

General Admission Tickets (GA):

Seating is limited and based on first-in, first-served. You may purchase up to 10 tickets online at a time. All GA tickets are available up until 15 minutes before setting sail unless sold out.

GA tickets allow you to move freely around the vessel, subject to level capacity restrictions.

GENERAL

We ask all guests to check the weather conditions and dress appropriately, including appropriate footwear.

Security reserve the right to refuse entry to any group or guest.

For further information or enquiries please email: bookings@seadeck.com.au

Frequently asked questions

How long do SEADECK Cruises go for?

SEADECK usually sets sail for 4 hours. For exact departure and arrival times please check the event info.

Are there 18+ age restrictions?

This is an 18+ event.

Is there a dress code?

We ask all guests to check the weather conditions and dress appropriately. We also ask no singlets, thongs, bridal paraphernalia or offensive clothing. We also ask no offensive tattoos and heavy jewellery. Closed toe sandals are permitted. Management reserves the right to refuse entry.

What happens if it's raining or bad weather?

On the rare occasion of extremely bad weather we will notify via social media & email should we need to cancel. However, we do endeavour to go out in the rain and have shade sails that cover majority of the vessel.

Do I need bring ID?

This is an all 18+ event. Valid form of ID upon boarding. Photos or copies of ID will not be accepted. Valid forms of ID are a current Australian Drivers License, an Australian or foreign passport or an Australian 18+ card.
